Our Services

{{fixture.0.service}}

Gas Compression Services

We specialize in gas compression solutions, ensuring the efficient and reliable transport of natural gas. Our services encompass the design, installation, maintenance, and optimization of compression systems.

Sales & Transmission Services

We facilitate the sale and transmission of natural gas, providing a vital link between producers and consumers. Our expertise ensures the seamless flow of gas through pipelines and distribution networks.

Plant & Terminal Maintenance

We offer comprehensive maintenance services for plants and terminals involved in the energy sector. Our maintenance programs are designed to enhance operational efficiency, safety, and compliance.

Pipeline, SVs & IPs Maintenance

Our pipeline services cover the construction, maintenance, and inspection of gas pipelines. We prioritize pipeline integrity to ensure the safe and reliable transport of gas.

We specialize in the maintenance of safety valves (SVs) and isolation points (IPs) within gas systems. These services contribute to system safety and reliability.

Meter Calibration Facility

We provide meter calibration services, guaranteeing accurate measurement of gas flow. Our precise calibration ensures compliance with industry standards and regulations.

Environmental and Regulatory Compliance

We provide guidance and support to ensure compliance with environmental regulations and safety standards, promoting responsible and sustainable energy practices.

Our commitment to excellence, safety, and sustainability underpins all our services. We continually invest in innovation and technology to deliver the highest level of service quality while minimizing environmental impact. Our goal is to be a trusted partner in the energy industry, helping clients achieve their objectives and contribute to a more sustainable energy future.

Gail (India) Limited

GAIL (India) Ltd was incorporated in August 1984 as a Central Public Sector Undertaking (PSU) under the Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas (MoP&NG). The company was initially given the responsibility of construction, operation & maintenance of the Hazira - Vijaypur -Jagdishpur (HVJ) pipeline Project. It was one of the largest cross-country natural gas pipeline projects in the world. Originally this 1800 Km long pipeline was built at a cost of Rs 1700 Crores and it laid the foundation for development of market for natural Gas in India.
